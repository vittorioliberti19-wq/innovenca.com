01 / About Us

Strategic partners
for Venezuelan industry.

INNOVEN C.A. is a Venezuelan company specialized in providing integrated industrial services and procurement of materials and equipment, with extensive experience in the industrial and energy sectors.

We have a team of highly qualified engineers, technicians and specialists, backed by partnerships with world-leading manufacturers.

We position ourselves as strategic partners to our clients, providing reliable, efficient technical solutions tailored to the operational demands of each project. Our approach is oriented to contribute directly to asset integrity, operational continuity and the sustainable economic benefit of our clients' operations.

Mission

Integrated solutions with technical commitment.

At INNOVEN, we provide integrated solutions in civil, mechanical, electrical and instrumentation engineering, as well as project procurement, with a firm commitment to quality, continuous improvement and customer satisfaction. We act with technical and professional responsibility, adapting to the challenges of the environment to guarantee reliable and efficient results.

Vision

To be a reference in multidisciplinary engineering.

To be recognized as a leading multidisciplinary engineering company in Venezuela, standing out for our operational excellence, adaptability and commitment to clients, consolidating long-term trust relationships in every project we undertake.
